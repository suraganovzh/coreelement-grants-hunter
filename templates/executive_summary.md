## Our Solution

**Core Element AI** has developed a proprietary **Probabilistic AI Modeling** platform that transforms mineral exploration from a subjective, time-intensive process into an objective, data-driven operation. Our technology integrates multiple geological data sources — geophysical, geochemical, and remote sensing — to generate probabilistic subsurface predictions at 1:10,000 scale.

## Proven Results

Our technology has been validated across **7 consecutive successful projects** with measurable outcomes:

- **$17M+ in total demonstrated savings** across exploration projects
- **79% reduction** in unsuccessful drilling campaigns
- **Prediction accuracy within ±15 meters** (US projects)
- Exploration timelines reduced from **years to weeks**
- **$1M+ savings per project** through optimized drilling programs

In our Mexico pilot project, we improved discovery probability from **0.5% to 75%** and reduced required drilling depth by **200 meters** — a transformative outcome that demonstrates the technology's commercial readiness.
